Owlbear Rodeo Tracker

It's a simple browser addon, letting you track dice rolls, combat turns, health and conditions in Owlbear Rodeo tabletop map simulator. Main features added by this extension are:

  • Combat tracker, that lets you track rounds and turns of the characters on the map.
  • Dice tracker, that lets you track who rolled what and when it happened.
  • Extension popup menu allows toggling modules on/off and keeps track of your games.

OBR Tracker works only with Owlbear Rodeo and depends on its updates. The latest OBR version that was tested with the extension is specified in OBRT popup menu.

OBR Tracker is developed independently, and is not officially endorsed by or affiliated with Owlbear Rodeo or its creators.

About the author

I'm just a D&D player, and after using Owlbear Rodeo with my party for a while, I felt that, however great it already was, OBR missed some features that are vital for our gameplay. So after a few sleepless nights I've made this.
